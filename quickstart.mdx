## Welcome to SFLUV

This guide will walk you through creating your account, requesting your first role, and taking your first action on the platform.

<Note>
SFLUV uses blockchain wallet authentication via Privy. You'll connect your wallet to create and access your account.
</Note>

## Step 1: Create Your Account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect Your Wallet">
    Navigate to the SFLUV platform and click "Connect Wallet". You can:
    - Connect an existing Ethereum wallet (MetaMask, WalletConnect, etc.)
    - Create a new embedded wallet through Privy
    - Use email login which generates a wallet for you
  </Step>

  <Step title="Complete Your Profile">
    After connecting, you'll be prompted to:
    - Enter your name
    - Provide your email address
    - Verify your email (check your inbox for verification link)
    
    <Warning>
    Email verification is required for most platform features. Make sure to click the verification link sent to your email.
    </Warning>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Explore the Merchant Map">
    Once logged in, you'll land on the merchant map showing all local businesses accepting SFLUV currency. You can:
    - Browse merchants by location
    - View merchant details and accepted payment methods
    - See your current account balance
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Step 2: Request Your First Role

All users start with basic access. To participate in workflows, you need to request specific roles.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Improver Role">
    **Improvers** complete workflow steps and earn currency.

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Navigate to Settings">
        Click on your profile menu and select "Settings" or navigate to `/settings`
      </Step>
      
      <Step title="Request Improver Status">
        Find the "Request Improver Status" section and click the request button.
        
        You'll need to provide:
        - Your motivation for joining
        - Skills or experience (optional)
        - References (if required by your community)
      </Step>
      
      <Step title="Wait for Approval">
        An admin will review your request. You'll receive an email notification when approved.
        
        <Info>
        Approval times vary by community, typically 1-3 business days.
        </Info>
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Proposer Role">
    **Proposers** create and submit community project workflows.

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Navigate to Settings">
        Go to `/settings` from your profile menu
      </Step>
      
      <Step title="Request Proposer Status">
        Click "Request Proposer Status" and provide:
        - Your organization or affiliation
        - Types of projects you want to propose
        - Relevant experience
      </Step>
      
      <Step title="Await Admin Review">
        Your request will be reviewed by community admins. Once approved, you can access the workflow builder at `/proposer`
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Merchant Role">
    **Merchants** accept SFLUV currency at their business.

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Add Your Location">
        Go to `/locations` and click "Add Location"
        
        Provide:
        - Business name
        - Address with coordinates
        - Business hours
        - Contact information
      </Step>
      
      <Step title="Request Merchant Approval">
        Submit your location for admin review. Include:
        - Business registration details
        - Payment receiving wallet address
        - Any relevant business licenses
      </Step>
      
      <Step title="Setup Redemption">
        Once approved, you'll receive:
        - QR codes for accepting payments
        - Access to transaction history
        - Merchant dashboard at `/wallets`
        
        <Warning>
        For W9 compliance (required to unwrap tokens to USD), you'll need to submit tax forms via the merchant status page.
        </Warning>
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Affiliate Role">
    **Affiliates** manage events and payouts for partner organizations.

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Request Affiliate Status">
        In Settings, click "Request Affiliate Status"
        
        Include:
        - Organization name
        - Organization mission
        - Event types you'll manage
      </Step>
      
      <Step title="Setup Your Organization">
        After approval, access `/affiliates` to:
        - Upload your organization logo
        - Create events
        - Generate redemption codes
        - Track affiliate balance
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Step 3: Take Your First Action

Now that you have a role, here's what you can do:

### As an Improver

<Steps>
  <Step title="Browse Available Workflows">
    Go to `/improver` to see your workflow feed. You'll see:
    - Approved workflows with available steps
    - Workflows matching your credentials
    - Bounty amounts for each step
  </Step>

  <Step title="Claim a Workflow Step">
    Click on a workflow to view details, then:
    - Review the step requirements and work items
    - Check if you meet any credential requirements
    - Click "Claim Step" to assign it to yourself
    
    <Info>
    Steps unlock sequentially. You can only claim the next available step in a workflow.
    </Info>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Start and Complete the Step">
    Once claimed:
    1. Click "Start Step" to begin work
    2. Complete all required work items:
       - Take required photos (camera-only if specified)
       - Provide written responses
       - Select dropdown options
    3. Submit your completed work
    
    <Warning>
    Make sure to complete all non-optional work items before submitting.
    </Warning>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Receive Payment">
    After supervisor approval (if required) and workflow completion:
    - Payment is automatically processed
    - Funds appear in your connected wallet
    - View payment history in `/wallets`
  </Step>
</Steps>

### As a Proposer

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Your First Workflow">
    Navigate to `/proposer` and click "Create Workflow"
    
    You can:
    - Start from scratch
    - Use a template from the library
    - Clone one of your previous workflows
  </Step>

  <Step title="Define Workflow Details">
    Fill in the workflow information:
    
    **Basic Details:**
    - Title and description
    - Start date
    - Recurrence (one-time, daily, weekly, monthly)
    
    **Roles and Steps:**
    - Create roles with credential requirements
    - Add sequential steps assigned to roles
    - Set bounty amounts for each step
    - Define work items (photos, responses, dropdowns)
    
    **Supervisor (Optional):**
    - Assign a supervisor to oversee completion
    - Set supervisor bounty
  </Step>

  <Step title="Submit for Voting">
    Once your workflow is complete:
    - Review all details carefully
    - Submit for community vote
    - Track voting progress at `/proposer`
    
    <Info>
    **Voting Requirements:**
    - 50% quorum must be reached
    - 24-hour countdown after quorum
    - Can be approved early if >50% of all voters agree
    </Info>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Monitor Workflow Progress">
    After approval:
    - View workflow status and step completion
    - See which improvers claimed steps
    - Track to completion and payout
  </Step>
</Steps>

### As a Voter

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Voting Queue">
    Navigate to `/voter` to see:
    - Pending workflows awaiting votes
    - Workflow deletion proposals
    - Current vote counts and quorum status
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review and Vote">
    For each workflow:
    1. Read the description and requirements
    2. Review steps and bounty amounts
    3. Check budget availability
    4. Cast your vote: Approve or Deny
    
    <Note>
    Your vote helps ensure quality projects that benefit the community and maintain budget sustainability.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Track Vote Outcomes">
    Monitor:
    - Quorum progress (need 50% participation)
    - Current approve/deny counts
    - Countdown timer after quorum
    - Final decisions
  </Step>
</Steps>
